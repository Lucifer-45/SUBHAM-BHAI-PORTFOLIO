About Me

I'm a dedicated QA Engineer with over 3 years of hands-on experience testing mobile apps, web platforms, and APIs. My passion lies in building seamless user experiences by ensuring software is stable, reliable, and bug-free. Throughout my career, I've been recognized for identifying high-impact bugs early in the development cycle, helping teams reduce bug leakage by up to 40%. I thrive in fast-paced Agile environments, working closely with developers, designers, and product managers to catch issues before they become costly. Whether it's crafting effective test strategies, performing exploratory testing, or collaborating on automated regression suites, I'm committed to delivering high-quality releases — faster and more confidently. For me, quality assurance isn't just about finding bugs — it's about building trust in every release.
